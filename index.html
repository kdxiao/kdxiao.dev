Observations

Here are some observations that shape my worldview:

  • Fact and opinion aren't complements, nor are they even well-defined concepts.
  • Everything you know is a projection by your senses onto consciousness. If you pay careful attention, thoughts, feelings, and perceptions are really the same type of thing.
  • Language is a game of applying measures or judgments to the world according to certain, often-unspoken rulesets. By nature, language can only point at the world through measurements qualified by such rulesets.
